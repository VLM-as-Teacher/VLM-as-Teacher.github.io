Method

A VLM Teacher for visual execution

Rather than rewriting a prompt, the teacher synthesizes task-specific reward queries and provides differentiable feedback to optimize a lightweight LoRA module in the VGM Reasoner.

Method overview placeholder Replace with your final framework figure assets/method/method_overview.png
1

Reward Query Synthesis

A VLM Teacher extracts final-goal and process-constraint queries from each rule-based task condition.

2

Online Optimization

Differentiable VQA losses backpropagate through intermediate video predictions to update only LoRA parameters.

3

Efficient Adaptation

Step distillation, surrogate decoding, frame sampling, and early stopping make test-time optimization practical.

Quantitative Results

Consistent gains beyond prompt refinement

Our method directly optimizes visual reasoning trajectories, substantially outperforming prompt-space refinement and sampling-based scaling at comparable test-time cost.
